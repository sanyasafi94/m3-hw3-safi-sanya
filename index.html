Who is Zhao 'Rosy' Lusi?

Zhao 'Rosy' Lusi is a Chinese actress and singer born in Chengdu, China. She began acting in 2018, working hard to do what she loved. Though she had been acting since 2018, she gained the most attention after starring in the famous show "The Romance of Tiger and Rose" in 2020.
